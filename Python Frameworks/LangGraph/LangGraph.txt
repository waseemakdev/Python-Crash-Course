What is LangGraph. Why its needed.? What was before of this?

LangGraph is an open-source framework developed by LangChain designed to build, deploy, and manage complex, stateful generative AI agent workflows.

It's need:
While linear frameworks like LangChain Expression Language (LCEL) are excellent for simple RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipelines, they struggle when tasks require reasoning, loops, and self-correction.
